== Wholesale Market Suite for WooCommerce Plugin, it provide that empowers your shop and give special privilege to them by setting product’s wholesale price with the product and category wise discounts according to quantity==

Wholesale Market Suite for WooCommerce Plugin in this plugin it provide the features of flexible discount on the product and category based on the quantity . In this plugin it manage the enable discount and pricing ,in this plugin provide the option to choose and apply discount on the Regular Price, Sale Price and Wholesale Price.In this there is a option to apply the discount on product based on pricing and category pricing also.Discount only apply within the quantity and choosen the product and category


=== How this plugin works on your WooCommerce Store? ===

It is quite simple to use this Wholesale Market Suite for WooCommerce  . Just enter the wholsale price on the edit product and then enable the discount option like product and category then add  the product or category to give discount on that then add the quantity of the product enables the discount and pricing like regular,sale and wholesale price.Then click the save button for data save .Then it reflect on the shop page and product page as well then add to cart the product within the limit to apply the discount after that the discount apply reflected on the cart table and checkout page also.

=== Benefits of Using this Plugin ===

 Wholesale Market Suite for WooCommerce provide the Benefits of product wise discount with the minimum and maximum quantity of the products and also give the Benefits of category wise discount according to the quantity .user enter multiple category in one time to give discount and same in products multiple product select at a time to apply the discount.

== Installation ==
= Automatic installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option as WordPress handles the file transfers itself and you don't need to leave your web browser. To do an automatic install of WooCommerce Order Coupon, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu and click Add New.

In the search field type "Wholesale Market Suite for WooCommerce" and click Search Plugins. Once you've found our Wholesale Market Suite for WooCommerce you can view details about it such as the point release, rating and description. Most importantly of course, you can install it by simply clicking "Install Now".

= Manual installation =

The manual installation method involves downloading our Wholesale Market Suite for WooCommerce  and uploading it to your web server via your favourite FTP application. The WordPress codex contains [instructions on how to do this here](http://codex.wordpress.org/Managing_Plugins#Manual_Plugin_Installation).

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How to add Wholesale Price =

In the product panel there will be a field named “Wholesale Price”. Admin has to enter wholesale price of product here.

= What is Wholesale Market Suite for WooCommerce =

Wholesale Market Suite for WooCommerce Plugin, it provide that empowers your shop and give special privilege to them by setting product’s wholesale price with the product and category wise discounts.

= How to give discount on product=
 
 To give discount on product go to the product tab and add product discount, quantity and save the product.It reflect on the shop and product page and enable the product discount.

 = How to give discount on category=
 
 To give discount on product go to the category tab and add category discount, quantity and save the category.It reflect on the shop and product page and enable the category discount.
